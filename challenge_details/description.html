This challenge comes from the paper Towards Realistic Single-Task Continuous Learning Research for NER. The paper presents a method of splitting the StackOverflowNER dataset into 5 train/test episodes to create a more realistic and challenging continual learning setting.

This challenge involves the "skewed" episodes from the paper. You should submit code to train a model on each of the 5 training episodes in succession, without revisiting prior episodes. The final model will be evaluated on all 5 test episodes, and the average F1 score over all 5 test episodes will be reported.
